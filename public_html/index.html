The Great Story

Our Journey

The idea of the restaurant started in our small village where my brothers and I sold some of the foods that our mother used to cook. After that, we decided to open our small restaurant in the village little by little until we opened this restaurant in Jeddah. We believe that food is from heaven, so we offer you the most delicious dishes that suit your taste and satisfy your hunger. We have the best chefs striving to satisfy you and deliver their best to you.
